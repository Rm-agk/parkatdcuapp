Glasnevin


Glasnevin is DCU's largest campus and is home to DCU Business School, the Faculty of Science and Health, the Faculty of Engineering and Computing, and parts of the Faculty of Humanities and Social Science such as the School of Communications and the School of Applied Language & Intercultural Studies (SALIS).

All Hallows


Dublin City University completed the purchase of the All Hallows campus in April 2016. The campus is used for office space for staff, lectures and student accommodation. Although the campus is in active use by the University during the academic year, Conference and Accommodation facilities are available during the Summer months, when many of the All Hallows legacy activities will take place. On-site catering for small to large groups is provided on campus.

St. Pats


DCU St. Patrick's Campus is a teacher training campus located 2.5km from Dublin City University, Glasnevin Campus. The campus has excellent and modern facilities including library, chapel, computer laboratory, WiFi and a sport centre complex.
